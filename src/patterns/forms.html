Checkboxes and radio buttons

Radio buttons

Sets of radio buttons must be wrapped in a fieldset for accessibility purposes. The legend element must carry the label for the set of buttons. This ensures that the question will be read out and associated with the answers for screen reader users.

Example

What kind of will would you like?

Fill in this form based on decisions you and the other person have agreed.

If you’re not sure, or need to speak to them first, leave the questions blank and we’ll go through them when we call.
