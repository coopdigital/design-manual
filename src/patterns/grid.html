Grid

Overview

We use a grid enable us to structure our designs. Just like our typography and vertical spacing, our grid allows the creation of consitant horizontal space across a variety of contexts.

Viewport sizes

The default Grid provides classes spanning 12 columns, and provides breakpoints at the following screen sizes:

  • All - 0px and up
  • Extra small - 320px and up
  • Small - 480px and up
  • Medium - 750px and up
  • Large - 960px and up
  • Extra large - 1300px and up

These settings can be overriden by specifying your own list of screen sizes you want columns available for. To keep the size of the resulting production stylesheet down, it is recommended to taylor the settings to your requirements, to make sure only the necessary classes are generated.

Columns and gutters

The default grid is based on 12 columns of equal width; 1 column for small and extra small viewports. The gutter of each column is a division of the 8px basline grid as follows:

  • Extra small - 8px
  • Small - 8px
  • Medium - 16px
  • Large - 32px
  • Extra large - 32px
