Design manual

When you're building a Co-op digital service, have a look in this design manual to find:

  • how to write good content
  • the building blocks of design such as interaction design patterns
  • the conventions we use for front-end development

Some of the advice is here because we've researched it and know it works. Other parts are our current best guess. Other parts are missing entirely because we don't know yet.

Terminology

A candidate is something we are exploring. Based on experience and best practice believe it will work for users but it may not yet have been fully research validated.

Candidate patterns are okay to use and, if you gain any user insight when running your testing please let us know.

Published means we are confident in the design pattern. It is fully documented, and validated with users.

Even published patterns can change or be added to over time. Depending on user needs there may be more than one published way of doing things.
