Terminology

A candidate is something we are exploring. Based on experience and best practice believe it will work for users but it may not yet have been fully research validated.

Candidate patterns are okay to use and, if you gain any user insight when running your testing please let us know.

Published means we are confident in the design pattern. It is fully documented, and validated with users.

Even published patterns can change or be added to over time. Depending on your user needs there may be more than one published way of doing things.
