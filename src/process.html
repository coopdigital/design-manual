What alpha, beta and live mean

Within our design system we label things as Alpha, Beta and Live.

You can use all the information in the design system in your service if we have choosen to include it. Alpha or beta design patterns or code should be rigorously user researched or tested.

Our status labels mean:

Alpha
This our first try at the guidelines, design pattern or code. We don't yet have any data to back up it's effectiveness.
Beta
We are partially confident in the guidelines, design pattern or code. Based on new information we may change this in the near future.
Live
We are fully confident in the guidelines, design pattern or code.
